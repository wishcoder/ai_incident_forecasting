Incident Details

Incident ID:005

Title:Router-2 Configuration Changes

Date and Time:2023-06-15 09:30

Reported By:Gregory Hines/IT Support

Component Affected:Router-2

Incident Description

During a scheduled update, configuration changes were applied to Router-2, inadvertently blocking external access to App-3 and App-4.

Impact Analysis

The misconfiguration resulted in a complete loss of service for external users of App-3 and App-4, affecting critical business operations.

Resolution Steps

1. The issue was detected through automated monitoring alerts.

2. IT Support initiated an emergency protocol to assess and rectify the configuration errors.

3. Configuration changes were rolled back to the last known good configuration at 10:15.

4. Full service was restored to App-3 and App-4 by 10:45, and a review was scheduled to prevent future occurrences.

Resolved By:Hannah Klein/Network Operations Team

Resolution Date and Time:2023-06-15 10:45

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

This incident emphasized the need for a more rigorous pre-deployment testing phase for any configuration changes, especially those affecting critical network infrastructure. It also highlighted the importance of having a quick rollback strategy for immediately addressing any issues that arise post-deployment.
