Incident Details

Incident ID:006

Title:Firewall-1 is not reachable

Date and Time:2023-07-22 14:05

Reported By:Isabel Martinez/Security Team

Component Affected:Firewall-1

Incident Description

Firewall-1 became unresponsive due to a suspected power supply failure, disrupting network traffic flow and access control for App-1 and App-2.

Impact Analysis

The downtime of Firewall-1 led to a temporary loss of external connectivity for App-1 and App-2, impacting user access and data security protocols.

Resolution Steps

1. The issue was immediately escalated to the hardware maintenance team.

2. A faulty power supply unit was identified and replaced by 15:30.

3. Firewall-1 was successfully rebooted, and full functionality was restored by 16:00.

Resolved By:Jason Clark/Hardware Maintenance

Resolution Date and Time:2023-07-22 16:00

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

The incident underscored the importance of regular hardware inspections and the need for having redundant power supplies for critical security infrastructure to prevent similar outages in the future.
