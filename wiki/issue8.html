Incident ID:008

Title:Firewall-1 Routing All Traffic to App-1

Date and Time:2023-09-14 08:30

Reported By:Marco Silva/IT Operations

Component Affected:Firewall-1

Incident Description

A misconfiguration in Firewall-1's routing rules directed all incoming external traffic exclusively to App-1, leaving App-2 inaccessible.

Impact Analysis

App-2 was completely cut off from external access, disrupting services for users dependent on it, while App-1 experienced an overload of traffic.

Resolution Steps

1. The routing misconfiguration was identified by the network team.

2. Immediate action was taken to correct the routing rules, restoring balanced traffic flow to both App-1 and App-2 by 09:15.

Resolved By:Sophia Loren/Network Team

Resolution Date and Time:2023-09-14 09:15

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

Emphasized the importance of validating configuration changes through a staging environment and conducting immediate post-deployment checks to ensure all services are operating as expected.
