Incident ID:015

Title:Service-2 Changes

Date and Time:2024-04-12 16:30

Reported By:Perry Cox/Development Team

Component Affected:Service-2

Incident Description

Recent updates to Service-2 introduced a misconfiguration, leading to data retrieval issues for App-1 and App-2.

Impact Analysis

The misconfiguration in Service-2 prevented Service-1 from accessing necessary data, directly impacting the operational efficiency of App-1 and App-2.

Resolution Steps

1. The development team was alerted to the issue and began immediate troubleshooting.

2. Service-2's recent changes were rolled back to the last stable configuration by 17:15.

3. Service functionality was fully restored, and a post-mortem analysis was scheduled to prevent similar issues.

Resolved By:Elliot Reid/Development Team

Resolution Date and Time:2024-04-12 17:15

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

Emphasized the critical importance of thorough testing and validation of service updates in a staging environment before deployment to production to ensure compatibility and functionality.
