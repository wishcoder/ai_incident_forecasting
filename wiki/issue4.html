Incident Report

Incident Details

Incident ID:004

Title:Router-2 is not reachable

Date and Time:2023-05-10 11:47

Reported By:Ethan Wright/Network Operations

Component Affected:Router-2

Incident Description

Router-2 became unreachable due to a suspected hardware failure, disrupting external connectivity for App-3 and App-4.

Impact Analysis

The loss of Router-2 connectivity halted all external traffic to App-3 and App-4, significantly impacting user operations and service delivery.

Resolution Steps

1. Immediate notification to the hardware team for diagnostics.

2. Temporary routing adjustments made to divert traffic through Router-1 while Router-2 was under investigation.

3. Hardware fault identified and Router-2 was replaced at 14:30.

4. After replacement, Router-2 was brought back online and normal service for App-3 and App-4 was restored by 15:00.

Resolved By:Fiona Kelley/Hardware Support Team

Resolution Date and Time:2023-05-10 15:00

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

The incident underlined the importance of having a robust hardware monitoring and failure detection system in place. It also highlighted the need for a quicker hardware replacement process and the effectiveness of having a contingency plan for rerouting traffic during critical hardware failures.
