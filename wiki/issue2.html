Incident ID:002

Title:Router-1 is not reachable

Date and Time:2023-02-20 09:32

Reported By:Alice Johnson/Network Operations

Component Affected:Router-1

Incident Description

Router-1 became unreachable at 09:32, preventing external access to applications App-1 and App-2. Initial diagnostics failed to identify the cause of the connectivity issue.

Impact Analysis

The unavailability of Router-1 has led to a complete loss of external connectivity for App-1 and App-2, affecting user access and critical business operations.

Resolution Steps

1. Network team alerted and began troubleshooting.

2. Restart Router-1 at 10:15 after initial diagnostics.

3. Connectivity restored, and services for App-1 and App-2 resumed normal operation by 10:45.

Resolved By:Bob Smith/Network Operations

Resolution Date and Time:2023-02-20 10:45

Status

Resolved

Lessons Learned

Further investigation revealed a firmware issue was the root cause. The incident underscores the need for regular firmware updates and more robust monitoring of network components to detect and address such issues proactively.
